Christus und die Ehebrecherin

Lucas Cranach der Jüngere

Jesus is depicted in the centre of the image in a blue robe with a red cloak. He points at the adultress with his right hand and holds her right lower arm in his left hand. She wears an orange-red dress and has lowered her head. A soldier holds her other arm. Another soldier stands in the foreground holding stones in his hands. More men stand behind the soldier facing towards Jesus.
